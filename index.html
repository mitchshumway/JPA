About Me

Hi! My name is Jeff Andrew. I’m an ambitious filmmaker with a passion for storytelling and collaboration. I specialize in commercial directing but am also am passionate about cinematography and editing. I love telling stories that make people feel something. Joy, sadness, fear, love, anger…you get the idea. I’m not too picky. I started out my career by working for a few different advertising agencies. I loved the constant challenge, but wanted more flexibility to pursue long term documentary projects and collaborate with more production companies so I jumped into the freelance world. I’m also a part 107 certified drone pilot. I’m available on a freelance/project basis worldwide. I am currently based in Boston, but travel for much of my work. Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you would like a custom reel or just want to chat! @ jeffpandrew@gmail.com Check out my Behind The Scenes page to see what I'm up to!
